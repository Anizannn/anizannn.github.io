Ambition

I'm Anizan Paul Smith — an Indian medical student who crossed borders to chase a calling. Born and raised in India, I made the deliberate decision to pursue my MBBS at Osh State University in Kyrgyzstan, embracing the challenge of studying medicine in an international environment far from home.

My long-term goal is to become a Cardiologist. The heart — its rhythms, its failures, its resilience — fascinates me both scientifically and humanistically. I believe great medicine requires curiosity, discipline, and a genuine connection with patients.

Outside of lectures and clinical prep, I document my journey through travel photography and personal storytelling — because the world a doctor sees shapes the doctor they become.

Sinus Rhythm. 72 BPM. All Systems Go.
